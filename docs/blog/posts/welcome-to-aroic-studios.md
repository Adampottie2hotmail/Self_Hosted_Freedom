### Why Self-Host?
✅ **Full Control** – Own your data and services.
✅ **Privacy & Security** – Reduce reliance on third-party platforms.
✅ **Customization** – Tailor your setup to fit your needs.
✅ **Cost Savings** – Avoid recurring fees for cloud services.
